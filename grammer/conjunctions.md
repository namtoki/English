##### and

- (原因から結果) It started to pour down, and we got soaked to the skin.

- (さすれば) Pick a fight with him, and you'll regret it.

##### so
- (原因から結果) I didn't use suncream, so I got burned.

- You didn't come to practice; therefore, you're out of the team.

### 逆行

#### 基本

##### but
- I linke my new boyfriend but hate his sense of dress.
- She never studies but always passes the tests easily.
- Excuse me, but can you keep the noise down, please?

##### 会話の中での but の応用
- A: How about a nice tie for your husband?
- B: But I'm not married!
- A: Just go up to her and ask her out. It's simple.
- B: Yes, but what if she says no?

##### not A but B
- He is not just my friend but my soul mate.

##### not only A but (also) B
- This car is not only powerful but (also) eco-friendly.

##### 前置詞として使われる but
- All but two of the climbers made it to the top.
- Look, I'm sure this is nothing but a phase your kid is going through.

#### however
- I know you don't want the flu injection. However, it's better to be safe than sorry.

#### yet
けれども。これで話は終わりではなくという気持ち
- Kelly was a criminal, yet many people admired him.

### 選択

#### 基本

##### or
- (もしくは) You can pay in cash, or you can use a credit card.
- (もしくは) Which color do you want, red or bule?
- (だいたい) 30 or 40 people attended the presentation.
- (さもなければ) Hurry up, or we'll miss the train.
- (さもなければ) We'd better do exactly as he told us, or else we'll be in deep water.
