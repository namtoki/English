# CONJUNCTIONS

## 等位接続

### 順行: 基本

#### and
- (時間的な流れ) I made a sandwich and ate it quickly.
- (原因から結果) It started to pour down, and we got soaked to the skin.
- (さすれば) Just pay a deposit, and you can take it home now.
- (さすれば) Pick a fight with him, and you'll regret it.

#### so
- (原因から結果) I didn't use suncream, so I got burned.

### 順行: 論理を追う (論文向き/固い)

#### hense
- Narumi spent 5 years in the States, hence her perfect English accent.

#### therefore
- You didn't come to practice; therefore, you're out of the team.

### 順行: 累加

#### on top of that
- They asked me to work extra hours and, on top of that, they docked my pay!

#### in addition

#### moreover

#### furthermore

### 順行: 例示

#### for example
- I like most Japanese food; for example, sushi, tempura, and soba.

#### for instance

#### :
- I learned a lot from my parents: honesty, loyalty, and curiosity.

### 順行: 明確化

#### that is
- I'm reading a book about Bushido, that is, the traditional code of the Japanese samurai.

#### in other words

#### that is to say

#### to put it another way

#### to be precise

### 逆行

#### but
- I linke my new boyfriend but hate his sense of dress.
- She never studies but always passes the tests easily.
- Excuse me, but can you keep the noise down, please?

##### 会話の中での but の応用
- A: How about a nice tie for your husband?
- B: But I'm not married!
- A: Just go up to her and ask her out. It's simple.
- B: Yes, but what if she says no?

##### not A but B
- He is not just my friend but my soul mate.

##### not only A but (also) B
- This car is not only powerful but (also) eco-friendly.

##### 前置詞として使われる but
- All but two of the climbers made it to the top.
- Look, I'm sure this is nothing but a phase your kid is going through.

#### however
- I know you don't want the flu injection. However, it's better to be safe than sorry.

#### yet
けれども。これで話は終わりではなくという気持ち
- Kelly was a criminal, yet many people admired him.

### 選択

#### or
- (もしくは) You can pay in cash, or you can use a credit card.
- (もしくは) Which color do you want, red or bule?
- (だいたい) 30 or 40 people attended the presentation.
- (さもなければ) Hurry up, or we'll miss the train.
- (さもなければ) We'd better do exactly as he told us, or else we'll be in deep water.

## 従属接続

### 条件

#### if
- If you wait a few minutes, I'll give you a ride.
- I know I can win if I play my best.

##### 高圧的な if 文
- (通常) If you say that again, I'll go home.
- (高圧) If you say that again, I go home.
- (高圧) If you say that again, I'm going home.

##### たとえ ... でも
- (可能性有) I don't care if you have no money.
- (非現実的) I don't care even if you have no money.

##### かどうか
- I don't know if Michelle received the package.
- He asked me if I have time for a drink.

#### provided / providing (that)
- Provided she can control her nerves, she has an excellent chance of winning.

#### on condition that

#### unless
- Unless something unexpected happens, the plan will remain unchanged.

#### as long as
- I'll love you as long as I live.

#### suppose / supposing
- Suppose she turns you down, what will you do?

### 考慮

#### given (that)
- Given the circumstances, a tie is a brilliant result.
