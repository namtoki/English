# ADJECTIVE

名詞を修飾

## 限定

FIXED: only, main, mere, chief, former

### 基本
- Look at the **cute** girl over there.
- This guy with at **goofy** face asked me out.
- These **rotten** eggs stink!

### 重ねて
- Mark is an **excellent** though **reckless** driver.
- Naomi is a **bright** but **lazy** student.
- Look at that **beautiful silk** top. // 限定詞 -> 感想/評価 -> 大きさ -> 新旧 -> 色 -> 材料 -> 名詞

### 自由に形容詞を作れる
- I have a **5-year-old** daughter.
- This is an amazing, **onece-in-a-lifetime** opportunity.

## 説明

FIXED: asleep, afraid, alive, awake, alone

### 基本
- My girlfriend is **gorgeous**.
- She is **beautiful and kind**.  // 接続詞を抜いてはいけない (限定のようにはできない)
- My parents were **strict but fair**.

### -body(one)
- I'm looking for somobody **better**.

### -thing
- I've tried everything **possible**.

### 最上級
- This is the worst scenario **imaginable**.

### There文
- I'm afraind there are no seats **available** for tonight's show.

### 欠乏感
- I was given a bag **full of cookies**!

### 数量表現
- That building is 150 meters **high** and 35 meters **wide**.
