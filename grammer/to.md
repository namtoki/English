# TO

## 主語 (一般的な内容, 規則など)
- To make new friends is not so easy.
- To talk in the library is prohibited.
- For anyone to drink and drive is unforgivable.

## 主語 (これから)
- To sell the house now would be crazy.
- To apologize to her is not such a bad idea, I think.

## 目的語
- I like to play video games with my buddies.
- I want to be a policeman.

### to 不定詞だけしかとらない動詞
- agree, decide, expect, hope, want, wish, promise, plan, mean, offer, refuse, manage, ,,,

### ing だけしかとらない動詞
- admit, deny, consider, imagine, enjoy, finish, stop, quit, give up, avoid, mind, miss, practice, suggest, recommend, ,,,

## come/get to
- He came to appreciate his parents.
- We got to know Tokyo very well.

## 説明型 (be 動詞)
- (一般的内容) To see is to believe.
- (これから) Our goal is to find a cure for AIDS.
- ★ (意図) If you are to get her back, you need to do more than just apologize.
- ★ (命令) You are all to be here by 7 a.m. sharp, understood?
- ★ (運命) 4 kids from Liverpool were to form the most famous rock band in the world.
- ★ (予定) The president is to visit Japan next week.

### ★ 説明型 (be 動詞以外)
慎重に結論付けている感じを出す
- The flight attendant seems/appears to be stressed out.
- Her prediction turned out to be right.
- His plan proved to be successed.
- We happend to be wearing the same dress!

## 目的語説明
慎重に結論付けている感じを出す
思考系の動詞でよく使われる
- I thought him to be an honest person.
- I consider him to be open-minded.
- I found her to be a most capable assistant.

## 動詞句の「足りないを補う」
- (目的) I'm going to Egypto to do some scuba diving.
- (原因) I was excited to hear about your new project.
- (結果) He grew up to be a famous architect.
- (根拠) She must be out of her mind to walk around such a dangerous area alone.

### 目的を明確に示すフレーズ
- I had to fill out loads of forms in order to get a visa.
- I suggest we leave around 6:30 so as to ensure we get good seats.

### 結果を強調するフレーズ
- We stood in line for over 2 hours, only to discover that it was the wrong line!
- One day he was here and the next he was gone, never to be seen again.

## 名詞句の説明
- I don't have the right tools here to fix your bicycle.
- (唯一の) Michelle was the first female golfer to play in a men's tournament.
- (-body) I need someone to drive me to the station.

## 形容詞の説明
- He is easy to fool.
- She is sure to lose her cool.
- The new recruits are eager to show their worth.

## WH + to
- I have no idea what to say.
- Tell me when to start.
- I know where to find them.
- Could you tell me which way to go for the post office?

## it + to
- It's difficult to get up in the morning.
- It's tough for the unemployed to make ends meet.
- I find it difficult to get up early.
- I thought it wise to let you know in advance.
