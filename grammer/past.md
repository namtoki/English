# PAST

## Position
修飾のみ

## Use Case

### 誰がやったのかわからない・言う必要がない
- The Egyptian pyramids were built over 4500 years ago.
- English is spoken worldwide.

### 頭でっかちな文を避ける
- I have been encouraged by the tremendous support I habe received from all my family and friends.

### 主語をそらさない

### 話に一般性を持たせる
- It is often said that English is a difficult language.

## COMBINATION

### TO 不定詞と受動文
- (TELL) I was asked to make a speech at my ex-student's wedding.
- (TELL) We were all told to stay calm.
- (THINK) One of last year's Nobel Prize winners is thought to be in jail.
- (THINK) Her new book is expected to become an immediate bestseller.
- (THINK) Jericho is said to be the oldest city in the world.

### GET
- John got arrested for harassment.
- I got asked for my autograph on the train.

## ADVANCED

### ★ 受動分を使わなくて済むケース
- These batteries charge quickly.
- These juice cartons open easily.
- Does your new car drive well?
- Green peppers don't peel easily.
- Our books are selling well

### もう一つの意味
