# COMPARISON

## 同等 (as ... as ...)

### 基本
- (形容詞) Tom is as tall as Mary.
- (副詞) She now speakes as naturally as a native speaker.

### ★ 感情の高ぶりを表す
- She visits her grandmother as often as 3 times a week.
- My brother can drink as many as 10 pints of beer without getting drunk!

### 限定語句 (not)
- I'm not as tall as him.
- She isn't as attractive as Sarah.
- He didn't play as well as his oppenent, so he lost.

### 限定語句 (almost)
- He is almost as tall as his Dad now.

### 限定語句 (数)
- This cake is not half as delicious as it looks.
- Facebook is more than twice as big as MySpace.
- Soon it may be three times as big!

### ★ 形容詞、副詞以外にも
- I have as many CDs as Ken.
- She is not as good at skiing as me.
- Eva didn't perform as well in the exam as Tess.
- I think readning a book is as exciting as going to Disneyland.
