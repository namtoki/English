# MOTIVATION (FEATURE)

---

## するために
- [x] `to V`                                ([Casual] するために) I left early `to` catch the train. / She studies hard `to` pass the test.
- [x] `so that S V`                         ([Neutral] するために) Mary spoke loudly `so that` everyone could hear her.
- [x] `In order (not) to V`                 ([Neutral] するために) I went to the train station `in order to` buy a ticket.
- [ ] `In order that S V`                   ([Formal] するために) Mary spoke loudly `in order that` everyone could hear her.
- [ ] `so as (not) to V`                    ([Formal] するために) She left home early `so as to` avoid traffic congestion.

## 目指して (長期的)
- [ ] `for the purpose of O`                ([Formal] [文語] する目的のために) I rented a P.O. box `for the purpose of` importing clothes.
- [ ] `with a view to V-ing`                ([Formal] を目的として) A fund was set up `with a view to` preserving our endangered planet.

## 願望
- [x] `hoping (that) S V`                   ([Casual] を望んで) I emailed her, `hoping that` she would reply.
- [ ] `in (the) hope(s) that S V`           ([Neutral] を望んで) I didn't phone till four o'clock `in the hope that` you'd be finished.

## 念のため
- [x] `just in case`                        ([Casual] 念のため) I'll write it down `just in case.`
- [x] `for a rainy day`                     ([Casual] 念のため) I decided to put some aside `for a rainy day.`
- [x] `in case S V`                         ([Neutral] に備えて) `In case` there's an earthquake, prepare a first-aid kit.
