# Verb, Adjective

- The hat `suits` you. (服装)
- it `fits` you. (サイズ)
- The blue tie `(matches | goes with)` you. (色合い)
- We live in Kamioka so Yokohama is easy to (`get to` | `access`).
- My boss is `stuck in his ways`.
    - stubborn
    - unwilling or unable to change
- I won't share my opinion if my wife seems as if she `is in a bad mood`.
