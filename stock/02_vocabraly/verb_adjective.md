# Verb, Adjective

## 似合う

- The hat `suits` you. (服装)
- it `fits` you. (サイズ)
- The blue tie `(matches | goes with)` you. (色合い)

## 通いやすい

- We live in Kamioka so Yokohama is easy to (`get to` | `access`).

- My boss is `stuck in his ways`.
  - stubborn
  - unwilling or unable to change
- I won't share my opinion if my wife seems as if she `is in a bad mood`.

## 断る

- (はっきり) They wanted me to participate in the meeting, but I `refused`.
- (不適切だ) The boss `rejected` my proposal.
- (丁寧に) He `declined` my invitation to dinner.
- (くだけて) She `turned down` his marriage proposal, though he looked like a nice guy.
