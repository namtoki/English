# Lesson

## 質問

- ... 点質問があります
  - I've got ... questions
    - So today I've got a couple of questions.
- 一番最初の質問は
  - the very first question is ...
    - the very first question is the use of to do versus -ing for "My hobby is..."
- と言うとき
  - when you say ...
    - when you say my hobby is
- どうすればよいか
  - what should I do ...
    - What should I do in a situation like this?
- どう表現しますか？
  - how would you say it?
    - If you were to state your dream, how would you say it?
  - What's it called?
  - What's the word for it?
- これは正しいですか？
  - is that true?
    - but is that true?
- と言ったら変ですか？
  - would it sound a little bit weird if I said ...
    - Would it sound a little bit weird if I said "My hobby is to take pictures"?
- と言えますか？
  - can you say ... ?
    - Can you say my goal is becoming a write?
- はどういう意味ですか？
  - What does ... mean?
- は ... と同じですか？
  - Is ... the same as ...?
- ～するもの
  - the thing that
    - The thing that you use to see the stars.

## 回答

- ～っても大丈夫
  - you can still ...
    - but you can still even with this one say my job is to take pictures of flowers

## 文構造表現

- 文章が続く
  - comes after ...
    - what comes after that has to be -ing like "Mmy hobby is taking pictures."
- 一番自然な表現は...
  - the most commonly accepted way is ...
    - but the most commonly accepted way is the -ing.
