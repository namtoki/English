# Vocaburaly

## Adverb
- `Such a`
  - That was `such a` fun day.
  - You are `such a` pro. (まさしく)
- `All kinds of ...`
  - I've been eating `all kinds of things`.

## Idioms
- one of those days
- `make the most of ..`
  - Let's `make the most of` it.
  - Let's `make the most of` our time here.
- `the best`
  - That show is `the best`!
- `something else`
  - That dinner was `something else`!
- `the only thing`
  - It was great! `The only thing` was the food.
  - Is that `the only thing` you think about?

## Work
- `ace` / `nailed it`
  - I think we `aced` that presentation.
  - I think I really `nailed it` this time.
- `work went late`
  - Sorry, `work went late`.
