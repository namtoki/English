# Work

## 通勤

- (通う) go to work
  - He goes to work by walk.
- (通いやすい) get to, access
  - We live in Kamioka so Yokohama is easy to get to.

## スケジュール

- 休みを取る
  - take off
    - take off two weeks
    - I'm going to take a week off from work this November. 
- 残業する
  - work late
    - She works late most of the days.
  - work went late
    - Sorry, work went late.
- 帰る時間
  - ETA (Estimated Time of Arrival)
    - I send her a message with my ETA.

## どうしているか (どうだったか)

- 達成する (した)
  - ace
    - I think we aced that presentation.
  - nail
    - I think I really nailed it this time.
  - hit it out of the park
    - We’ve hit it out of the park!
- うまくやる ⇔ 衝突する
  - get along ⇔ clash
    - My wife did not get along with the principal of her school.
- 要領を得た ⇔ 的外れの
  - to the point ⇔ beside the point

## 数字について

- 半端ない
  - Off the charts
    - His guitar performance was totally off the charts!
- 突き抜けている
  - Through the roof
    - My stress was through the roof
- 増やす
  - increment
  - boost
    - I wanted to boost sales this month, so I created a new team.
  - elevate
    - If you want to elevate your performance, you're gonna have to practice.
  - amplify
    - To amplify sales, we're gonna have to come up with a game plan.

## 役職

- senior management | upper management
  - It is easier to share my opinions when senior management is not present.

## リモートワーク

- 見えます？聞こえます？
  - Can you see me?
  - Can you hear me?
- ミュートにしてました
  - I was on mute.
- 見えない
  - I can't see you/the material/the notes.
- 大きくして
  - Could you make the material/notes bigger?
- 文字にして
  - Could you type it in the chat, please?

## 未分類

- 決まり
  - standard procedure
    - This is just a standard procedure. (念のためです)
- 副業
  - side hustle
- 文化
  - culture
    - The company culture is different.
- 合う
  - be for
    - That job wasn't for me.
- 外資系
  - foreign owned
  - be based in
    - My company is based in U.S. but 80% of the employees are Japanese.
