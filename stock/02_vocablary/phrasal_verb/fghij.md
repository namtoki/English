# Phrasal verb (FGHIJ)

## get

- get up
  - (起きる) He gets up at 7 AM.
- get ready
  - (準備する) It takes him about an hour to get ready for work.
- get to
  - (イライラさせる) The construction in front of my house is so noisy. It gets to me.
  - (イライラさせる) It’s raining the whole week! This weather is getting to me.
  - (気に障る) Don’t let it get to you. It’s really nothing.

## give

- give off
  - (発する) A woman passed by me giving off a subtle scent of perfume.

## go

- go over
  - (チェックする) Good morning, everyone. We should get started. First, I'd like to go over the most recent marketing data.

## guess

- guess ... right
  - (言い当てる) I guessed the age right.
  - (言い当てる) I couldn't guessed those right.

## hang

- hang on
  - Hang on a sec.
- hang in
  - Hang in there. ≒ Good luck.
