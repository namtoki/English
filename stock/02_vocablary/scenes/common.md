# Common

## 〇〇なもの

- the best
  - That show is the best!
- something else
  - That dinner was something else!
- one of those days
- the only thing
  - It was great! The only thing was the food.
  - Is that the only thing you think about?
- the rest of ...
  - You can have the rest of them.
  - I don't want to do this for the rest of my life.
- the only way to ...
  - The only way to get to karaoke is to keep working. (やるしかない)
  - This is the only way to live. (やっぱりこれでしょ)

## 趣味

- passion
  - It's my new passion. (最近の趣味)

## その他

- seniors
  - The people who disagreed are old fashioned seniors.
