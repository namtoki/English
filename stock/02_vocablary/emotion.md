# Emotion

## 強い衝撃

- 立っていられなくなる
  - weak at the knees
    - Mary went weak at the knees when she saw the man.
    - Hearing the shocking news made me go weak at the knees.

## 感じ方

- 面白い
  - interesting (興味深い)
  - intriguing (超興味深い)
- 機嫌が悪い
  - be in a bad mood
    - I won't share my opinion if my wife seems as if she is in a bad mood.
- 明らかな
  - black and white
    - Things in life are never black and white.
  - unambiguous
    - The warnings were unambiguous.
- 雑な (いい加減な)
  - sloppy
    - The bridge collapsed soon after completion because of the sloppy construction work.
    - This is a sloppy job. Do it over.
- やり過ぎな (度が過ぎている)
  - go too far
    - He went too far there.
  - (go | be) over the top
    - I went over the top at my child's sports day.
    - I think painting every wall in the house bright yellow is a bit over the top.
