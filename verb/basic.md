# Verb (Basic)

## GO
- Basic
    - me -----------------> over there
    - 悪い方向 (come と比較して)
    - He **went** to the museum.
- deriv.
    - (死ぬ) Poor Granny has **gone.**
    - (辞める) You are simply not up to the job. You have to **go**.
    - (通用する) As far as dress code is concerned, anything **goes.**
    - (変化する) The milk **has gone** sour.

## COME
- Basic
    - me <----------------- over there
    - よい方向 (go と比較して)
    - Where do you **come** from?
    - Mmm... nothing exciting **comes** to mind.
- deriv.
    - (もうすぐ来る) the **coming** year/trend.
    - (するようになる) I **came to** really like spicy food.
    - (どうして) **How come** I wasn't inveted?

## RUN
- Basic
    - There's a free shuttle bus that **runs** between the airport terminals.
    - That movie made shivers **run** down my sprine.
- deriv.
    - (稼働している) Who left the tap **running** ?
    - (稼働している) I have a **runny** nose.
    - (運営する/仕切る) He gave me tips on how to **run** a meeting.

## BRING
- Basic
    - COME + もの
    - I'll **bring** my guitar.
    - (もたらした) The Internet **has brought** many changes to our everyday lives.
- deriv.

## CARRY
- Basic
    - 支える
    - That's a heavy backpak you're **carrying.**
- deriv.
