# USAGE (VERB)

- [助動詞] 疑問詞と共に用いる should:   「いったい」「そもそも」という意になる
- [前置詞] 動詞 of:                     writes `of the distant past` / hear `of A` / know `of A`
- [使役動詞] have A done:               I had my hair cut.
- [語法] help (to) 原形動詞:            The brochure helped me find the information
- [語法] inform A of B:
- [語法] inform A that 節/疑問詞節:
