# Weather

## 天気予報

- 予報では ... だった
  - ... was predicted
    - Snow was predicted this morning.

## 晴れ

- 晴れ
  - sunny

## 雨

- 濡れる
  - get wet
    - I got wet on my way to work.
- びしょ濡れ
  - be soaked
    - I'm soaked with sweat.

## 気温

- 肌寒い
  - chilly
- 寒い
  - cold
- 凍える
  - freezing

## 湿度

- じめじめ
  - humid, humidity
    - Seattle has low humidity in summer.
    - Tokyo is too humid.
    - It is humid in the summer but it is cold and dry in the winter.
