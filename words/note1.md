## 困難
- `difficult`       ([Formal] 知的に複雑な難しさ)       The instructions were difficult to follow.
  - `difficulty`    ([Neutral] 困難、難しさ)            He had no difficulty finding a job.
- `hard`            ([Neutral] 努力を要する難しさ)      The test was hard.
  - `harden`        ([Neutral] 硬くなる、硬くする)      The clay will harden in the sun.
  - `hardship`      ([Writing] 苦難、困難)              They faced many hardships during the war.
  - `hardworking`   ([Neutral] 勤勉な)                  He is a hardworking student.
  - `hardy`         ([Writing] 頑丈な、耐寒性の)        This is a very hardy plant.
- `tough`           ([Casual] 忍耐力を要する難しさ)     He's going through a tough time.
- `challenging`     ([Neutral] やりがいのある難しさ)    This project is very challenging.
  - `challenge`     ([Neutral] 挑戦、難問)              I'm ready for a new challenge.
- `complex`         ([Writing] 構造が複雑な難しさ)      The human brain is an incredibly complex organ.
  - `complexity`    ([Writing] 複雑さ)                  I was amazed by the complexity of the design.
- `complicated`     ([Neutral] 入り組んで分かりにくい)  The instructions were too complicated.
  - `complicate`    ([Writing] 複雑にする)              Don't complicate the situation.
  - `complication`  ([Writing] 面倒な事態、合併症)      The surgery was successful, but there were complications.
- `tricky`          ([Casual] コツがいる、油断ならない) The last question on the test was tricky.
- `arduous`         ([Writing] 骨の折れる、過酷な)      It was an arduous journey through the mountains.
- `strenuous`       ([Writing] 肉体的に激しい)          Avoid strenuous exercise for a few weeks.
