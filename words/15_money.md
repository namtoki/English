# Money

This category contains words related to money, spending, earning, and financial activities.

## Words

### Noun
- `profit` /ˈprɒfɪt/                ([Neutral] 利益)                The profit was higher than expected.
- `reward` /rɪˈwɔːd/                ([Neutral] 報酬、褒美)          Hard work brings its own reward.
- `allowance` /əˈlaʊəns/            ([Neutral] 小遣い、手当)        My parents give me a weekly allowance.
- `spending` /ˈspendɪŋ/             ([Neutral] 支出)                Government spending has increased.

### Adjective
- `lucrative` /ˈluːkrətɪv/          ([Writing] 傲かる、有利な)      He has a lucrative business.
- `profitable` /ˈprɒfɪtəbəl/        ([Neutral] 利益の出る)          The company had a profitable year.
- `wasteful` /ˈweɪstfəl/            ([Neutral] 無駄遣いの)          That's a wasteful use of resources.

### Verb
- `spend` /spend/                   ([Neutral] 使う、費やす)        I spend too much on clothes.
  - `splurge` /splɜːdʒ/             ([Casual] 散財する)             I decided to splurge on a new car.
- `waste` /weɪst/                   ([Neutral] 無駄にする)          Don't waste your time on this.
- `squander` /ˈskwɒndə/             ([Writing] 浪費する)            Don't squander your money on useless things.
  - `fritter` /ˈfrɪtə/              ([Writing] 浪費する)            He frittered away his inheritance.
  - `blow` /bləʊ/                   ([Casual] 浪費する)             He blew all his money at the casino.


- `heritage` /ˈherɪtɪdʒ/             ([Neutral] 遺産)          We must preserve our cultural heritage.
