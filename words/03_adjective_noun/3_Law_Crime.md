# Law and Crime

## Law
> `compliance` /kəmˈplaɪəns/        ([Neutral] 遵守、コンプラ)      The company ensures compliance with regulations.
  - `observance` /əbˈzɜːrvəns/      ([Formal] 遵守、実行)           Observance of the law is mandatory.
  - `adherence` /ædˈhɪrəns/         ([Formal] 遵守、忠実)           Adherence to policy is required.
  - `conformity` /kənˈfɔːrməti/     ([Formal] 遵守、適合)           Conformity to standards is essential.
  - `obedience` /oʊˈbiːdiəns/       ([Neutral] 服従、遵守)          Obedience to the rules is expected.
  - `abidance` /əˈbaɪdəns/          ([Writing] 遵守、継続)          Their abidance by the law was expected.
> `acquittal` /əˈkwɪtəl/            ([Legal] 無罪判決)              The acquittal surprised everyone.
  - `exoneration` /ɪɡˌzɑːnəˈreɪʃən/ ([Formal] 無罪放免、潔白証明)   His exoneration came after new evidence.
  - `vindication` /ˌvɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/   ([Formal] 潔白証明、弁護)       The verdict was a vindication of her claims.
  - `pardon` /ˈpɑːrdən/             ([Formal] 恩赦、許し)           The governor granted him a pardon.
  - `absolution` /ˌæbsəˈluːʃən/     ([Formal] 免罪、赦し)           He sought absolution for his mistakes.
  - `discharge` /dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒ/       ([Legal] 免責、釈放)            The court ordered his discharge.
